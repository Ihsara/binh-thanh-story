The rhythm of a hub day

When does each corner wake, eat, and go quiet? Each clock reads morning / noon / evening — the wedge radius shows intensity (full = busiest), colour shows the hub's character. The top dish is named below each clock.

These clocks show an editor's read of when each corner feels busy — authored editorial intensity on a 0–2 scale, not a measured footfall or traffic count.
