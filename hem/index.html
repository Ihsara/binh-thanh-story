Quận Bình Thạnh · an address-depth story

The boulevard sells;
the hẻm lives.

Read me your address and I can tell you how deep into the city you live.

In Hồ Chí Minh City a street address is a path. Each slash is a turn off the last road into a narrower one — the hẻm, the alley. The number of slashes is the address's depth: how far the front door sits from the boulevard.

Hẻm 290/56/13/34 Nơ Trang Long  —  off Nơ Trang Long, into alley 290, then 56, then 13, to house 34: depth 3.
