A map of the map

How OpenStreetMap learned Bình Thạnh

For years the district's alleys were nearly invisible to OpenStreetMap — a few dozen named hẻm. Then, in 2019, a mapping campaign poured in: the count of named alleys jumped from tens to nearly two thousand in a single year. This is not the city growing. It is the map catching up to a city that was already there.
