The Anatomy of Centers

Bình Thạnh's real centers of place-life — found from all 25,368 mapped places, not 49 chains, and named by the landmarks people actually use.

    Method & honesty

    Centers are the peaks of a draw-weighted density field over every mapped place. The weight table (below) is a design choice — it up- weights destinations (markets, cinemas, food, culture) over incidental services. Disclosed, not truth. The weighting reshapes each center's character; it barely moves a center's location — Bình Thạnh is uniformly walk-dense, so the same 13 peaks emerge either way. Territories reuse the walk-network partition (every street awarded to its nearest center). Over-index = a center's domain share ÷ the district baseline. Dots in the explorer are anonymous (coordinates only).

    Two honesty notes. Real-estate services genuinely rival coffee in Bình Thạnh — about 1,370 storefronts against ~1,230 coffee shops — and it is a stable count, not a data artifact (its net change across the archive is flat to slightly negative). The môi-giới-nhà-đất shopfront is simply that common here. And a “lean” is a soft signal: a center tilts toward a domain without a domain dominating it — read it as a whisper, not the strong “quarter” label.
