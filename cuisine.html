Where the food is

Every place that names its dish, mapped across Bình Thạnh. Colour = kind of food; isolate one to see where it gathers.

Dishes are detected from each place's name (phở, bún, cơm, bánh mì, …); foreign cuisines from the Overture category. Only the food that announces itself is classed — a “Quán Ăn” with no dish in its name stays in Eateries. Not a census of every kitchen. Method →

Places: Overture Maps (CDLA-Permissive-2.0). Streets: OpenStreetMap.
